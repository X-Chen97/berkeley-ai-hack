LiMg₁₄BiO₁₆ is Caswellsilverite-derived structured and crystallizes in the tetragonal P4/mmm space group. The corner-sharing octahedra are not tilted. There are two shorter (2.19 Å) and four longer (2.22 Å) Li-O bond lengths. There are four inequivalent Mg²⁺ sites.
